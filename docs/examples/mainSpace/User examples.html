Validation of airborne waveform lidar simulations for a complex 3D savanna in Australia

Code, models, data and instructions are provided for lidar simulations. This document is a work in progress.

1. Building mixed tree-grass scenes

Generating tree and grass object files

OnyxTree tree models:
- direct parameterisation in OnyxTree
- adjustment of LAD to known distribution
- material mapping of leaves
Onyxtree grass models:
- matched to scene estimates of mean height and cover
- random distribution assumed
Scene objects:
- testing random vs. clumped distribution of trees

Validation of generated scenes

- ability to replicate measured CCP-FPC and SBA-FPC relationships
- comparison of measured and modelled gap size distributions
